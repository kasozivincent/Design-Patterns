Design Patterns

Design Patterns have been around in our industry even before the 1990s. In 1994, a group of four smart engineers decided to document the then recurring patterns that they continously faced daily in their designs in their seminal book, Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object Oriented Software.

Since the title of the book was so long to pronounce, developers gave it the funny name of Gang of Four (GoF) since it was written by four authors.

Reasons to consider Design Patterns

  • Help you create proven designs

  • Enhance your understanding of Frameworks

  • Create a vocabulary between designers
